Slugcat Oath

I swear always to be loyal to Slugelot

To accept the slugginess that is a slugcat

At 12:05 on the third of every month, I shall dine on waffles and bananas

I accept that I am a poisonous potato

Meow boing, meow boing

Explanation of the Slugcat Oath

line one- See the Slugcattian Religion page to learn who Slugelot is

line two- Seems pretty straightforward

line three-  It is not that important when you eat your monthly waffle and your monthly banana.  Just try to eat at least one of each every month, and as close to 12:05 on the third as possible. (My personal record is 11 minutes late on the third)

line four- a long time ago me and my friend were trying to figure out what Minecraft item most closely resembles a slugcat.  I jokingly suggested poisonous potato, and it stuck.

Line five-  Meow is a cat sound and boing is sort-of a slug sound
